Cabbage rolls are a beloved traditional dish in many cultures, typically made with cabbage leaves stuffed with a savory filling of meat, rice, and spices. They are simmered in tomato sauce for a rich and comforting flavor.

Ingredients

  • Large cabbage leaves
  • Ground meat (beef, pork, or a mix)
  • Cooked rice
  • Onions and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs

Steps

  1. Blanch the cabbage leaves in boiling water to soften them.
  2. Mix the ground meat, cooked rice, onions, garlic, and seasonings to prepare the filling.
  3. Place a spoonful of filling on each cabbage leaf and roll it up tightly.
  4. Arrange the rolls in a pot and cover them with tomato sauce.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 1-2 hours until fully cooked and tender.
  6. Serve hot with sour cream or fresh herbs on top.
