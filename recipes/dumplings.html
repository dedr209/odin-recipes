Dumplings are a beloved dish found in many cultures, consisting of dough filled with various savory or sweet fillings. They can be boiled, steamed, or fried, offering a versatile and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water or milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Filling of your choice (meat, potatoes, cheese, or fruit)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, water, eggs, and salt until smooth.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut it into small circles.
  3. Add a spoonful of filling to each circle and fold the dough to seal the edges.
  4. Boil the dumplings in salted water until they float to the top.
  5. Serve hot with sour cream, butter, or your preferred sauce.
