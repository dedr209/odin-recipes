Bortsch – A Taste of Tradition

Bortsch is a traditional soup originating from Eastern Europe, known for its rich flavor and vibrant red color, thanks to its main ingredient – beetroot. It is often served with sour cream and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Beetroot
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Meat or vegetable broth
  • Sour cream and fresh herbs for serving

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables: peel and chop the beetroot, carrots, potatoes, and onions.
  2. Sauté the onions and carrots in a pot with a bit of oil until soft.
  3. Add the beetroot and broth. Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the potatoes and cabbage. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream and fresh herbs on top.
